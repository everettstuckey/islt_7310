Artifact: Claims vs Evidence Mini-Lab

A short activity where students compare financial news claims with data sources.

Procedure

  1. Collect 3 headlines on a topic.
  2. Identify claims and implied causation.
  3. Locate primary data; assess validity.

Students produced quick write-ups; discussions emphasized bias recognition.
