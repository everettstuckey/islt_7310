Quick Read

The drafts are not two versions of the same layout. Claxton is closer to a legacy manual compilation, with source standards, job descriptions, ethics text, and a provider directory included in the manual body. The current draft is a redesigned operating handbook. It retains the core program concepts but expands implementation controls, procedures, role boundaries, evaluation routines, and appendices.

The most important review question is not whether every Claxton page appears verbatim. It is whether historical artifacts from Claxton should remain in the handbook body, move to a source/reference file, or be replaced by current controlled district sources.

Claxton Material Not Reproduced Verbatim

  • Acknowledgement from Dr. Henning and committee member list.
  • Board assurances as a distinct historical subsection.
  • Large inserted K-12 Missouri Learning Standards School Counseling tables under the old section title.
  • Full elementary and secondary job descriptions with legacy payroll/personnel details.
  • Full ASCA Ethical Standards text reproduced in the manual.
  • Mental health and adolescent substance abuse provider directory with named agencies and phone numbers.
  • Blank or minimally populated end pages after the provider directory in the extracted PDF.

Current Draft Additions Or Expansions

  • Purpose and use page that explains how the handbook should function as an operating manual.
  • Explicit Missouri CSCP alignment section with framework elements and SLPS implementation use.
  • Grade-by-grade implementation emphasis and detailed scope-and-sequence appendices.
  • Annual administrative agreement, advisory council, program calendar, needs assessment, use-of-time analysis, data review, and annual results report.
  • Appropriate and inappropriate duty guidance, fair-share duties, and full implementation duty protection.
  • Procedures for referral, crisis and safety response, mandated reporting, schedule and credit review, transfer intake, family engagement, community partnerships, records, and controlled procedure references.
  • Collaboration guidance for counselors working with social workers, school psychologists, nurses, administrators, and teachers.
  • Manual maintenance expectations, board policy source requirements, and annual policy calendar.
  • Operational forms for ICAP, transcript review, senior exit, PROBE, referrals, SST, functional behavior assessment, behavior intervention, individual safety planning, and annual results reporting.

Recommended Use

Use this comparison as a review map. The current draft appears broader and more operational. Claxton is useful for historical continuity and for checking whether older artifacts need preservation. The next review should decide which Claxton artifacts belong in the handbook itself and which belong in a source/reference companion file.
