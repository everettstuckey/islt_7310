Artifact: Annual PL Goal Cycle with Evidence

This artifact documents my annual professional learning (PL) goals aligned to district priorities and student needs. Goals are S.M.A.R.T., connected to measurable outcomes (e.g., increased scholarship application submissions), and reviewed quarterly.

Goal Summary

  • Improve scholarship navigation outcomes by 20% by redesigning information architecture and adding in-context prompts.
  • Adopt accessibility-first design patterns across counselor web resources (semantic HTML, keyboard support, WCAG AA contrast).
  • Integrate lightweight analytics for ethical, privacy-preserving feedback loops.
