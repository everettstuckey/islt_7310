Artifact: Ethical Analytics Brief

A 1-page brief translating seminar readings into practical guardrails for counselor-facing analytics.

Guidelines

  • Minimize data; collect only what is necessary to improve resources.
  • De-identify and aggregate at all times.
  • Be transparent with users (plain-language notices).
  • Prefer on-device or zero-PII solutions.

Application

Implemented count-based metrics on scholarship pages and removed any third-party tracking. Posted disclosure on relevant pages.
