GOOGLEFINANCE YTD Information

4. Obtain YTD Stock Information

To get the Year To Date (YTD) stock information:

=(GOOGLEFINANCE(ticker,"price")/INDEX(GOOGLEFINANCE(ticker,"close","01/01", today()),2,2))-1

Example for Apple Inc.:

=(GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","price")/INDEX(GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","close","01/01", today()),2,2))-1

How to Use GOOGLEFINANCE for Currency Conversion

To get live currency conversion rates directly in Google Sheets:

Replace the stock ticker with "Currency:currency1currency2". For example, to find the conversion rate between U.S. dollars and Canadian dollars:

=GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDCAD")
