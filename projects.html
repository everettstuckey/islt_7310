Side Projects

The SLPS Learn & Earn Internship Program (Portfolio Model) aims to place students in paid internships at St. Louis employers operating in highly competitive career fields including STEM. As an employer, you will host a student intern who will work on targeted projects to grow their expertise and create a portfolio of completed products as proofs of their hard skills.
