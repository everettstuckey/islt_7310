Market Value Assets give shape to the World of Work

The Kauffman Foundation in Kansas City has framed this cogently: every student should leave our school district with Market Value Assets.

My addendum would be: and at least one no-cost postsecondary option to pursue their goals.

InternshipsAuthentic job tasks at a worksite or approved location under qualified supervision.
Client-connected projectsAuthentic problems solved with professionals for an employer or community need.
Entrepreneurial experiencesLearners research, prototype, implement, reflect on, and adapt solutions.
Regionally vetted IRCsIndustry-recognized credentials validated for regional workforce needs.
College creditNine or more hours of college-level credit completed toward a degree.
