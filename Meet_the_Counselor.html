Meet the Counselor

My name is Everett Stuckey, and I am the high school counselor at McKinley CLA. Currently, I am an EdS candidate in the school of Information Science and Learning Technologies at the University of Missouri. I hold a Master of Arts in Counseling from Lindenwood University and a Bachelor of Science in Finance from Indiana University, where I was a Herman B Wells Scholar. This scholarship covered my full tuition, room, board, and stipend, allowing me to graduate debt-free.

My goal is to help students achieve their full potential by providing guidance, resources, and support. I am passionate about education and committed to making a positive impact on my students' lives.

I am relaxing on my summer vacation by launching an internship program for the Saint Louis Public Schools Foundation and teaching a course on Data Analytics and Digital Media in the IT & Cybersecurity Summer Academy.
